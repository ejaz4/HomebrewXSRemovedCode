EntropyUI doesn't collect any data.
We believe in a private and secure system, without worrying about trackers, or ads in the OS.

However, if you install third-party software, there may be apps or trackers that we cannot disable. We recommend you to use open-source software and to use your PinePhone's kill-switches.



Privat information
(User account information, location data, contacts, browser history, credit card information, usage time, web searches, cookies, GPS, Bluetooth connection history, Wi-Fi hotspot information, Speech regognitation)
will stay on your device. You are in control. We are not able to see any of your data. What'S on your device, stays on your device.

HomebrewXS Search Privacy

Our search homepage is stored locally on our System, if you search for something for device will connect to the DuckDuck Go servers, by this point, your Search Data may be stored in their servers for a while. We are not able to hide any of your search data. DuckDuck Go is anyway the safest and most private search engine you can use. Every word you type into HomebrewXS Search stays on your PinePhone, We cannot see any of your searches.

SupernovaOS and EntropyUI is designed to protect your privacy. It offers a beautiful and secure GUI.
You can make this world a safer place, we don't need companies that track us, just to offer ads. SupernovaOS is free forever.
